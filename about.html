Brand Overview

Inspired by Mathura's Heritage — Crafted with Purity

Radha Rani Milk & Delight is a premium traditional Indian sweet shop inspired by the dairy heritage, purity, and cultural sweetness of Mathura — the land of Lord Krishna.

The Mathura Connection

The trusted legacy of Radha Rani Rasgulla

Mathura occupies a singular place in India's culinary and spiritual landscape. Known as the birthplace of Lord Krishna, the city is equally celebrated for its deep-rooted dairy traditions. Radha Rani Milk & Delight carries forward the proud association of Radha Rani Rasgulla — a name synonymous with trust, quality, and authenticity in Indian sweets.

Our Core Values

Purity (Shuddhta) is the non-negotiable foundation

Premium Dairy Ingredients

Milk is sourced with care from reliable local dairies; chhena is made fresh daily from pure milk.

Fresh Khoya & Pure Ghee

Khoya is slow-reduced in-house and ghee is pure, traditionally churned, and never adulterated.

No Shortcuts

No preservatives, no adulterants, no milk powder shortcuts — just pure, honest ingredients prepared with care.
